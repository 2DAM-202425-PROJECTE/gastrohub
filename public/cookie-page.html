Details of the Cookies Policy

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites store on the user's browser to improve their browsing experience. They allow the site to remember information about the user's visit, such as selected preferences, previously visited navigation items, and even login credentials for easier access in future visits. Cookies play a crucial role in ensuring the efficient functioning of the website and providing a more personalized experience. Additionally, these cookies may be temporary, disappearing when the browser is closed, or permanent, remaining on the user's device for a specified period.

Types of cookies used

On our website, we use several categories of cookies to provide an optimal service:
Essential cookies: These are crucial for the basic operation of the website, ensuring that essential elements like navigation or access to secure features function properly.
Analytics cookies: These cookies collect anonymous data on how users interact with the website, such as which pages are visited most often, which sections are less consulted, or the time spent on each page. This information helps us improve the content and structure of the site.
Customization cookies: These cookies remember personal preferences like the selected language, the user's region, or visual settings, providing a more consistent and convenient browsing experience.

How to manage cookies?

Cookie management can be done through the browser settings. Users have the option to allow, block, or delete cookies according to their preferences. This setting may vary depending on the browser used, but it can generally be accessed through the "Settings" or "Preferences" section. It is important to note that disabling some cookies may negatively impact the browsing experience, preventing access to certain features or making content personalization more difficult. We recommend reviewing and adjusting the settings periodically to ensure they meet your needs.
