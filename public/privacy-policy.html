Privacy Policy

At GastroHub, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our customers and ensuring

that their personal information is handled securely and responsibly. This privacy policy outlines

how we collect, use, and share the information of users of our restaurant application and

associated services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that users provide when they sign up for our application,

place orders, make reservations, or interact with us. This information may include:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Delivery address (for home orders)
  • Payment information (processed through secure third-party systems)

As you interact with our application, we may automatically collect information such as:

  • Device details (device type, operating system, etc.)
  • IP addresses
  • Information about your visits to our application (pages viewed, time spent, etc.)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

-Loyalty Programs: To manage the points and rewards system for customers.

-Security: To ensure data security and prevent fraud.

-Communication: To send you updates about your order, notifications about offers and promotions, and respond to your inquiries or requests.

-Enhancing Your Experience: To customize the digital menu and tailor recommendations according to your preferences.

-Providing Services: To manage orders, reservations, and the point of sale service.

3. Sharing Your Information

We will not share your personal information with third parties without your consent, except

in the following cases:

-Service Providers: We may share your information with payment and logistics service providers that assist us in managing orders and payments.

-Legal Obligations: If required by law or to respond to legal requests.

4. Security of Your Information

We take appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized

access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. User Rights

Users have the following rights regarding their personal information:

  • The right to access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • The right to request restrictions on the processing of your information.
  • The right to withdraw consent for data processing.
  • The right to request data portability.

You can exercise these rights by contacting us through the details provided below.

6. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our application uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience and analyze traffic.

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices.

We will notify you of any significant changes via a notification in the application or through your email.
